Schedule for the Club

MONDAY

    UPPER-BODY

  • Bench Press – 4x8
  • Pull-ups or Lat Pulldown – 4x10
  • Dumbbell Shoulder Press – 3x12
  • Triceps Dips – 3x10
  • Bicep Curls – 3x12
  • Core: Plank – 3x30 sec

TUESDAY

    Lower Body Strength

  • Squats – 4x8
  • Deadlifts – 3x6
  • Lunges – 3x12 (each leg)
  • Leg Press – 3x10
  • Calf Raises – 3x20
  • Core: Russian Twists – 3x20

WEDNESDAY

    Rest or Active Recovery

  • Incline Dumbbell Press – 4x10
  • Overhead Press – 4x8
  • Lateral Raises – 3x15
  • Tricep Pushdowns – 3x12
  • Push-ups – 3 sets to failure

THURSDAY

    Pull Day (Back, Biceps)

  • Barbell Row – 4x8
  • Dumbbell Rows – 3x10
  • Face Pulls – 3x15
  • Hammer Curls – 3x12
  • Core: Hanging Leg Raises – 3x10

FRISDAY

    Pull Day (Back, Biceps)

  • Romanian Deadlifts – 4x10
  • Goblet Squats – 3x12
  • Leg Curls – 3x15
  • HIIT: 15–20 mins (sprints, bike intervals, etc.)
